About

The UNZA Student Results Hub allows registered students to securely access, download, and email their exam results directly from the University of Zambia database.

Features

  • Secure login with username and password
  • Download exam results as PDF
  • Email exam results directly to your registered email
  • Responsive and easy-to-use interface

How to Use

  1. Login with your student credentials.
  2. Click on “Print Results” to download your PDF.
  3. Click on “Email Results” to receive a copy in your email.
